# AI use
Some of the code here was written with AI assistance (specificaly Claude
by Anthropic). I directed it, reviewed it, and I'm responsible for it.
Saying otherwise would be dishonest. I have enough experience to know
when the output is good and when it isn't, and to push it towards
something worth shipping. The ideas are my own - the work is genuinely
collaborative, but probably not groundbreaking.

## Resource cost
Running AI uses real electricity and water. Those costs land
on communities near data centers and on the climate. I can't offset that
individually, but I'm not going to pretend it isn't happening.

## Training data
The models I use were probably trained on a mix of work that
was shared freely and work that wasn't. That's a real problem with no clean
individual fix. Being upfront about using the tools is the best I can do.

## Unethical means, ethical ends
Using something with a known cost to do
something useful doesn't erase the cost - both things are just true. I've
weighed it and chosen to proceed.

## Content concerns
If you think your work ended up in a model I used, or that something here
reproduces your work without credit, please open an issue.

## Discussion
I am happy discuss any of the above.
